OVERVIEW

Originally established as a specialized provider of technological services and solutions, Ridhitek was limited to the card, payment, and lending industries. With our next-generation digital services and consultancy, we help clients in 15 countries navigate their digital transition.

We professionally guide our clients through their digital transformation projects thanks to our more than ten years of experience managing systems and working for financial institutions, third-party payment processors, and fintech companies. We are able to create exceptional project delivery experiences and performance levels never before seen thanks to our deep domain expertise, technological know-how, and application level competence in the three areas. Positively outrageous client delight is the result.
